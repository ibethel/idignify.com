I. Dignify is DEVELOPING a network of people and various organizations to ban together in the fight against human trafficking and modern day slavery to bring about global justice. They are raising up and training caregivers to aid and work with survivors of the sex trafficking industry. Their mission is to equip communities in effectively combatting the multiple justice issues affecting society. Striving to open the eyes of the local and global community to the severity and oppressive world of modern day slavery, I.Dignify is raising it's voice to bring freedom to the captives.

Bob and Kimberly Johnson are the founders and directors of I. Dignify who are passionate in bringing change and permanent freedom to victims of modern day slavery. A life altering encounter with a fifteen year old girl rescued out of the sex trade in Cambodia caused Bob and Kimberly to take a drastic shift from the city of San Francisco to worldwide focus. Their heart is to see people become who they were truly created to be and assist in revealing identity to those that had it stolen from them.
